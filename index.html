Planned Projects

League of Legends Calculator

I hope to develop a simple calculator for predicting the outcome of combat in the online multiplayer video game League of Legends.

League is a complex game where players can fight while moving, where combat effectivness is highly situational and subject to a large number of rules, and where the correct strategey is to advance on the enemy when you expect to win, and to retreat when you expect to lose. The ability to asses a combat situation and determine if it is a winning fight or a losing fight is thus and essential skill in League. This is why developing a calculator for answering this question would be such an interesting project.

To start, I will develop the capability to evaluate simple combat under constrained conditions. Next I will branch it out to cover more of the rules and conditions, but still with strict assumptions about the combatants' combat performance and decision making, such assuming every attack will hit, or that both opponents will waste no time in between moves or attacks. After that, I will add the functionality to let users arbitrarily modify assumptions like these, and see how that would influence who wins and by how much.
